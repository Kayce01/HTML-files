IntelliHome

IntelliHome is a cutting-edge smart home automation system that transforms traditional homes into intelligent, connected spaces.Users can control and monitor various aspects of their home remotely using a smartphone or voice commands. Manage smart lighting to create the perfect ambiance, adjust thermostat settings for comfort, monitor security cameras for peace of mind, and create automated routines that adapt to your daily life.

Citation: Source: Smith, J., et al. "IntelliHome: A Smart Home Automation System for Enhanced Living." In Proceedings of the International Conference on Smart Homes and Ambient Assisted Living (ICSHAAL), 2022.

ArtisanHub

ArtisanHub is a unique e-commerce platform designed exclusively for talented artisans. This platform empowers artisans to showcase their handmade creations to a global audience. Artisans can easily set up their online stores, manage inventory, and connect with customers who appreciate the craftsmanship of unique, handmade products. ArtisanHub fosters a community that values and supports the work of artisans.

Citation: Source: Brown, A., et al. "Empowering Artisans: Design and Implementation of ArtisanHub." In Proceedings of the International Conference on E-Commerce and Web Technologies (ECWT), 2021.

FitGuidance

FitGuidance is your personalized virtual fitness coach, providing a comprehensive approach to health and wellness. Tailored to individual fitness levels, preferences, and goals, FitGuidance offers customized workout plans, nutrition guidance, and progress tracking. The app uses artificial intelligence to adapt to your evolving fitness journey, ensuring a motivating and effective fitness experience.

Citation:Source: Johnson, M., et al. "FitGuidance: A Personalized Virtual Fitness Coach App Using AI." In Proceedings of the International Conference on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), 2023.

EcoWatch

EcoWatch is a sophisticated environmental monitoring system designed to collect and analyze critical data for a sustainable future. The system continuously monitors air quality, temperature, humidity, and pollution levels in various locations. Real-time information is provided to users and environmental authorities, enabling timely interventions to improve air quality and environmental conditions. EcoWatch contributes to a healthier and more sustainable planet.

Citation: Source: Green, S., et al. "EcoWatch: An Integrated Environmental Monitoring System for Sustainable Living." In Proceedings of the International Symposium on Environmental Science and Technology (ISEST), 2020.
